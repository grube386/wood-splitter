# Wood Splitter — Premium Website

## What This Is

A super premium single-page website for Wood Splitter (www.woodsplitter.si), a Slovenian manufacturer of excavator-mounted hydraulic log splitters. The site showcases the BOWS 20 flagship product, builds brand credibility, and drives visitor inquiries — all in three languages (Slovenian, English, German) switchable without page reload.

## Core Value

Visitors instantly understand that Wood Splitter is the premium, safest, and fastest excavator-mounted log splitter on the market — and can contact the company effortlessly.
